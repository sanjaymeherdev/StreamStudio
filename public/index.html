Frequently Asked Questions

Do I still need OBS or streaming software?

Yes! Stream Studio receives RTMP streams from OBS, XSplit, vMix, or any encoder. Your camera, mic, and game capture stay in your streaming software. Stream Studio adds overlays and distributes to multiple platforms.

Does Stream Studio have a camera/PIP option?

No. Stream Studio does NOT capture camera or encode video. It only receives an existing stream and injects overlays. Your camera is handled by OBS or your streaming software.

What overlays can I add?

Animated GIFs, PNG logos, BRB scenes, Starting Soon screens, donation alerts, follower notifications, chat messages, and custom text overlays.

Does re-encoding affect quality?

No! Stream Studio uses pure passthrough for the main video. Overlays are composited without re-encoding the entire stream, so there's no quality loss.

Can I run this on free hosting?

Yes! Deploy to Render free tier (with self-ping keep-alive) or use a cheap VPS. The lightweight design uses minimal resources.

How many platforms can I stream to?

Unlimited! Stream Studio can forward to any number of RTMP destinations simultaneously — YouTube, Twitch, Facebook, Kick, Trovo, or custom servers.
